How to Play
×
The Setup
Add your players, then choose Locations, Biomes, Historical Eras, or Movies. Each round picks one category and gives the Jester a different role.
The Modes
Role Mode shows each player a role for the chosen word. Word Mode hides the role label and shows only the shared word for everyone except the Jester.
The Clues
Take turns saying one clue related to the secret word, location, or biome. Be clear enough to seem informed, but not so clear that you give it away.
The Trial
Discuss and vote. If the group picks the Jester correctly, the Cast wins. If not, the Jester escapes and wins. If the Jester is caught, they may still guess the word to steal the round.
